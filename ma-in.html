Ma'in Hot Springs
Relax and recharge at the natural hot springs

In the hills just above the Dead Sea lie the famous Jordanian thermal springs, or Hammamat Ma'in. This hidden oasis is home to a scenic hot spring waterfall that overlooks the oriental-style Ma'in resorts which include spas and thermal pools with water that reach up to 40Â° celcius. There are a number of offers on the internet and on site for relaxing tours around Madaba-Mount Nebo. The Ma'in Hot Springs are a great, cost-effective and accessible destination for locals and tourists for a weekend getaway.They offer a special way to connect and reconnect with authentic Middle Eastern culture and life-style.
