Wadi Rum
Desert tours and adventures

The Wadi Rum desert is a stunning destination for those looking to capture the natural scene in Jordan. It is especially a site worth seeing in the spring time where the night sky is clearest. brightest and full of stars that reveal numerous constellations. Additionally, for anyone interested in the cinematic world, a number of movies were filmed in Wadi Rum, inlcuding Dune, The Martian, Transformers - Revenge of the Fallen, 2 Star Wars movies and Lawrence of Arabia. For anyone who visits Jordan, a trip down to Wadi Rum is a must.

Activities and Destinations Within
  1. Sandboard down Al Hasany dunes
  2. Visit Seven Pillars of Wisdom
  3. Ride a camel
  4. Take a hot air balloon tour
  5. Buggy through the desert
  6. Visit a bedouin camp or go glamping
Best Time to Visit
Spring March-May
Autumn September-November
